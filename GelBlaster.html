What Are Gel Blasters?

Gel Blasters are eco-friendly, safe alternatives to traditional airsoft or paintball guns, using water-based biodegradable Orbeez for ammo.

Orbeez were originally developed for agricultural purposes to help plants retain water and improve harvests. The unique properties of the superabsorbent polymers used to make Orbeez were later used in consumer products.

Gel blaster guns are toy guns that fire small, water-soaked polymer balls similar to Orbeez. They're often used in recreational target shooting and games similar to paintball or airsoft. The gel balls are non-toxic, biodegradable, and burst on impact.
